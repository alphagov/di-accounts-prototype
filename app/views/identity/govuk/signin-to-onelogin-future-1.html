Using your GOV.UK One Login

GOV.UK One Login is new. It is a first step towards creating a single place where you can:

  • access all services on GOV.UK
  • prove your identity to any government service that needs to check who you are

GOV.UK One Login does not work with all government accounts and services yet (for example Universal Credit).


Services you can use with GOV.UK One Login

At the moment you can use your GOV.UK One Login to access these services:

  • Apply for a vehicle operator licence
  • Apply to become a registered social worker in England
  • Find a lost teacher reference number (TRN)
  • GOV.UK email subscriptions
  • LITE (Licensing for International Trade and Enterprise)
  • Personal tax account
  • Recruit an apprentice
  • Register for a National Professional Qualification (NPQ)
  • Register to vote
  • Request a basic DBS check
  • Sign your mortgage deed

If you do not have a GOV.UK One Login yet, you can create one when you use one of these services for the first time.

If you’re looking for a different service, sign in to another government account.

Sign in to your GOV.UK One Login

Sign in to:

  • see and access the services you’ve used with your GOV.UK One Login
  • change your sign in details (email address and password)
  • change how you get security codes
  • update your saved identity information (if you’ve proved your identity with GOV.UK One Login before)
  • delete your GOV.UK One Login
