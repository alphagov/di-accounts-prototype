About GOV.UK One Login

GOV.UK One Login is new. It is a first step towards creating a single place where you can:

  • access all services on GOV.UK
  • prove your identity to any government service that needs to check who you are

Services you can use with GOV.UK One Login

GOV.UK One Login is new. It is a first step towards creating a single place where you can:

  • Apply for a vehicle operator licence
  • Apply to become a registered social worker in England
  • Find a lost teacher reference number (TRN)
  • GOV.UK email subscriptions
  • LITE (Licensing for International Trade and Enterprise)
  • Personal tax account
  • Recruit an apprentice
  • Register for a National Professional Qualification (NPQ)
  • Register to vote
  • Request a basic DBS check
  • Sign your mortgage deed
