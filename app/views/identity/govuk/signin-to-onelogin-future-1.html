About GOV.UK One Login

GOV.UK One Login is new. It is a first step towards creating a single place where you can:

  • access all services on GOV.UK
  • prove your identity to any government service that needs to check who you are

GOV.UK One Login does not work with all government accounts and services yet (for example Universal Credit).


Sign in to your GOV.UK One Login

Sign in to:

  • see and access the services you’ve used with your GOV.UK One Login
  • change your sign in details (email address and password)
  • change how you get security codes
  • update your saved identity information (if you’ve proved your identity with GOV.UK One Login before)
  • delete your GOV.UK One Login
