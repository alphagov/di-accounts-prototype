Change your details or delete your GOV.UK One Login

You can sign in to your GOV.UK One Login to:

  • change your sign in details (email address and password)
  • change how you get security codes
  • update your saved identity information
  • delete your GOV.UK One Login

If you delete your GOV.UK One Login

This will permanently delete the:

  • sign in details for your GOV.UK One Login
  • identity information saved in your GOV.UK One Login

It means you will no longer be able to access the services you’ve accessed with your GOV.UK One Login in the past.

It will not delete the personal information held by services you’ve used.

If you need a GOV.UK One Login in the future, you’ll have to create a new one.
