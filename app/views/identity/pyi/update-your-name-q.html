Are you sure you want to update the name saved in your GOV.UK One Login?

At the moment, updating the name saved in your GOV.UK One Login can take up to 5 working days.

You’ll need to contact the GOV.UK One Login team using an online form.

They will delete your identity information and you’ll need to prove your identity again.

You need to have a UK passport or driving licence under your new name to change it in your GOV.UK One Login.

If you need to use the service now

GOV.UK One Login can still confirm your identity and you can access the service without updating your name.

However, the service will be given the name currently saved in your GOV.UK One Login.
