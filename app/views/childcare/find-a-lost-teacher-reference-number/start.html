Find a lost teacher reference number (TRN)

Use this service to:

  • find your teacher reference number (TRN) if you do not know it or have forgotten it
  • check if you have a TRN

Before you start

Have your National Insurance number ready, as we'll ask for this.

You need a GOV.UK One Login to sign in to this service.
Start now

Other ways to find your TRN

Your TRN may be shown on a:

  • payslip
  • teacher pension statement
  • teacher training records

The TRN has previously been known as a QTS, GTC, DfE, DfES and DCSF number.

Fina a lost TRN by phone

Call the Teaching Regulation Agency, and have your National Insurance number ready:

Telephone: 020 7593 5394
Lines are open Monday to Thursday, 9am to 5pm, and on Friday from 9am to 4:30pm (excluding public holidays)
