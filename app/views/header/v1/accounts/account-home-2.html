Services you can use with your GOV.UK One Login

You can use your GOV.UK One Login to access some government services (for example your childcare account or your personal tax account).

Services you can use with your GOV.UK One Login

These are all the services you can use with your GOV.UK One Login:

  • Apply for a vehicle operator licence
  • Apply to become a registered social worker in England
  • Find a lost teacher reference number (TRN)
  • GOV.UK email subcriptions
  • Licensing for exporting controlled goods (LITE)
  • Personal tax account
  • Recruit an apprentice
  • Register for a National Professional Qualification (NPQ)
  • Register to vote
  • Request a basic DBS check
  • Sign your mortgage deed

There are some government services that you cannot use with your GOV.UK One Login yet (for example Universal Credit).

In the future, you'll be able to use your GOV.UK One Login to access all services on GOV.UK.
