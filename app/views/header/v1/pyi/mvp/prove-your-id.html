Prove your identity

This takes about 10 to 20 minutes.

We’ll ask for your current address and passport details.

We’ll also ask you some security questions only you will know the answer to. You might find it easier to answer these questions if you have information about:

  • your mobile phone contract
  • your bank account
  • any credit cards, loans or mortgages you may have

Your proof of identity will be saved to your GOV.UK One Login. This means you can skip the identity questions next time a service needs to check who you are.
