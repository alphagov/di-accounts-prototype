Services you can use with your GOV.UK One Login

These are all the services you can use with your GOV.UK One Login:

  • GOV.UK email subscriptions
  • Personal tax account
  • Renew your driving licence
  • Register to vote
  • Request a basic DBS check
  • Tax your vehicle

There are some government services that you cannot use with your GOV.UK One Login yet (for example Universal Credit).

In the future, you'll be able to use your GOV.UK One Login to access all services on GOV.UK.
